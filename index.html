Hi, my name is Andrew Rosero. I am a Visual Communication Student at San Francisco State University. In my free time I am also a freelance illustrator and designer.

I love being creative and learning new ways to apply my creativity. I have been an artist since elementary school, and from then I knew that art was my passion.
I was born in San Francisco and raised in Daly City, where much of my love for my heritage was cultivated.
